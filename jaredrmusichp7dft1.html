Generosity & Negotiation (Part 2)

Mastering Elegance, Generosity & Negotiation had some of the most emotional songs when it came to limerence. The original album featured 13 songs and introduced listeners to the red era. The lead single was "Arizona" while "Obsessed With You" became the most streamed song from the project. Written while seeing red, the album explored jealousy, longing, obsession and heartbreak surrounding one unforgettable girl. What was never meant to become a series is now continuing with a brand new 15-song sequel.
