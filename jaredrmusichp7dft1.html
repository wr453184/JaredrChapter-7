Limerence Killed the Hopeless Romantic (April 2026) (Deluxe: June 2026)

Artist · Writer · Producer

Limerence Killed the Hopeless Romantic is the 7th Studio Album by Jared R. Jared is an R&B Pop Artist from Virginia. This album is the full explanation and disclosure of the difference between a "Limerent Romantic" vs a "Hopeless Romantic". The songs on the Album are based around 3 love interest, and in they reality will never happen, but the thought of "what if" is more comfortable than taking action vs taking a chance and facing failure. The album is for told to have the best songs, and introduce songs with a new style, while still having some songs that hold on to the older and more familiar style. Expanding the audience, but not loosing the existing audience. Introducing elements of Rap and hip-hop to the existing traditional pop and R&B elements. Jared's Official Board of Song Reviewers, rate the album with excellence. Late-night Piano and guitar sessions bring us amazing content. From honest lyrics and vibe-heavy, chill melodies to his extensive set of raw vocals and top-of-the-line adlibs, with songs like Playboy Heart, Golden, Me and you, and In my head. This album is sure to win old and new fans over.
